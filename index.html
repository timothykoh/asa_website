Welcome to the home page of the Asian Students Association of Carnegie Mellon University, one of the largest students organizations on campus. We're always eager to meet new people and make new friends, so please add yourself to our email list and come out to our general body meetings at 5:30 on Wednesdays @ UC Danforth.

What Makes ASA Lovin'?

ASA is comprised of students interested in exploring Asian culture, joining discussion of race-related issues, and participating in different events throughout the school year, our organization is a fun way to get involved and meet new people. We welcome anyone who is interested in being a part of our family, so feel free to come out!

Campus Involvement

ASA participates in a long list of campus events, including Culture Night, Relay For Life, Multicultural Late Night, 1000 Plus, Wavin’ Flags, Volleyball Tournament, ECAASU, Ski Trip, Valentine’s Day Date Auction, themed club parties, banquets, formals. We also organize our own social events such as BBQ, S’Mores Night, Half-Off Late Nights, snack breaks and study groups!

Booth

ASA is the current winner for booth: a competition to build the best two storey structure for CMU’s Spring Carnival, and one of our biggest activities of the year. All general body members, board members, and even alumni will come out and help build an amazing booth, which will hopefully win first place again! Through the times we spend together building our booth and organizing other events, ASA members develop close friendships that last a lifetime.
